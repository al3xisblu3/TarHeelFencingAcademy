Weekly Schedule

Introductory Package

“Foundation for Future Champions”
Schedule: Monday & Wednesday 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Begin your journey into fencing the right way, laying a solid foundation is essential for success in this highly technical and strategic sport. Jumping straight into advanced training without learning the basics can leave new fencers feeling lost and overwhelmed.

Our Introductory Package provides a structured group training environment where beginners learn the essential fundamentals including proper footwork, blade control, parrying, and attacking. Under close guidance from experienced coaches, each fencer builds confidence, develops correct habits, and gains the skills needed to transition smoothly into higher-level group and competitive classes.

Competitive Package

“Path to Excellence”
Schedule: Monday, Tuesday, & Wednesday 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Designed for fencers with established fundamentals, the Competitive Package is tailored for athletes aspiring to compete at regional, national, and international levels. This program features advanced technical and tactical training, physical conditioning, and individualized performance plans guided by world-class coaches.

Fencers will gain experience in competition strategy, mental preparation, and bout analysis all within a high-performance training environment that fosters discipline, growth, and excellence.

Private Lessons

  • Lessons are one on one with the coach for 20 or 40-minute intervals.
  • Club members can sign up for lessons using their online accounts.
  • Cancellation is 48hrs for all private lessons.

Clinics and Camps

  • Clinics and camps are taught by our elite coaching team consisting of National, International, and Olympic coaches.
  • Camps are offered for students aged 5 and up of all ability levels and are divided by weapon, age, experience, and USFA rating.
