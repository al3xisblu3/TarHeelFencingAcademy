About Our Club

Located in the Fetzer Building at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, our fencing club is dedicated to teaching the ancient art of fencing to fencers of all skill levels.

Our experienced coaches and welcoming community make us the perfect place to start your fencing journey or take your skills to the next level.

Our Programs

Beginner Classes

Learn the basics of fencing in a fun, supportive environment. No experience necessary!

Competitive Team

Join our competitive team and represent Fetzer Fencing in regional tournaments.

Open Fencing

Practice your skills during our open fencing sessions. All experience levels welcome.
